❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about TravelNest.

Yes! TravelNest is completely free to use. All features including the Budget Planner, Trip Generator, and destination tracking are available at no cost. Your data is saved locally in your browser.

All your data (saved budgets, wishlist, visited destinations, newsletter subscription) is stored in your browser's localStorage. This means it stays on your device and is not sent to any server. Clearing your browser data will remove it.

The Destination of the Day uses JavaScript to calculate the day number of the current year, then uses that number to select a destination from our list. This means the same destination is shown for the whole day, and a new one appears automatically each morning.

The ambient sounds are generated entirely in your browser using the Web Audio API, which is built into all modern browsers. No audio files are downloaded. We use different types of synthesised noise filtered to sound like waves, rain, or city traffic.

Yes! TravelNest is built as a Progressive Web App (PWA). After your first visit, the pages and scripts are cached by the service worker. You can install it on your home screen and use most features without an internet connection (destination images still require internet).

The travel costs shown are approximate estimates based on typical travel budgets. Actual prices vary greatly depending on the time of year, your booking choices, and current exchange rates. Always check up-to-date prices before booking your trip.
